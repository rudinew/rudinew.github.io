EFFICIENCY OF APPLICATION OF OF ANTI-WISHED CHEMREAGENTS IN WINTER MAINTENANCE OF MOTORWAY ROADS AND AERODROMES


ABSTRACT

Gameliak I.P, Dmitriev N.N., Popelysh I.I. Koritchuck S.O., SemenchenkoO.V. Efficiency of application of of anti-wished chemreagents in winter maintenance of motorway roads and airlines. Visnyk National Transport University. Series «Technical sciences». Scientific and Technical Collection. - Kyiv: National Transport University, 2020. - Issue 1 (46).

This article refers experimental research the effect of anti-ice chemicals on ice melting temperaturewith using thermal imaging equipment.

Research object- ice melting processes under the influence of anti-ice chemicals.

The purpose of work is experimental research the impact of anti-ice chemicals on ice melting temperature and setting of the indicator of complex efficiency of chemicals for the winter maintenance of automobile roads and aerodromes.

Research methods - experimental scientific-research.

The results can be used by road and airport pavement exploitation in winter period.

KEYWORDS: CHEMICALS, AN INDICATOR OF INTEGRATED PERFORMANCE, MELTING TEMPERATURE.
