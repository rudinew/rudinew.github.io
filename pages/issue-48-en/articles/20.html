FIBER OPTIC INFORMATION TRANSMISSION SYSTEMS

ABSTRACT

Malysh M.I., Kulish M.R. Fiber optic information transmission systems. Visnyk National Transport University. Series «Technical sciences». Scientific and Technical Collection. - Kyiv: National Transport University, 2021. - Issue 1 (48).

Features of information transmission on optical fibers in the presence of spark and electric noise are investigated in detail.

The purpose of research: to determine the requirements for light sources, optical cables and photodetectors most suitable for the manufacture of fiber optic systems suitable for information exchange.

The object of research is fiber optic systems.

Research method is theoretical.

The basic requirements for the choice of light-emitting diodes, photodiodes and optical cables in the technical implementation of optical communication in the presence of spark and electromagnetic interference.

KEYWORDS: LED, OPTICAL LINE, PHOTODETECTOR, INFORMATION.
