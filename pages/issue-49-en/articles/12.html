FEATURES OF THE COMPETITIVENESS ASSESSMENT OF THE NATIONAL ECONOMY OF UKRAINE IN THE CONTEXT OF GLOBALIZATION

ABSTRACT

Lozhachevska O.M., Grechan P.Y. Conceptual principles of innovative development of enterprises. Visnyk of National Transport University. Economic Sciences Series. Scientific and technical collection. - Kyiv: National Transport University, 2021. - Issue 2 (49).

The article is devoted to substantiation of scientific and organizational bases on maintenance of innovative development of the enterprises.

The purpose of the work is to generalize the existing provisions and substantiate the directions of further development of the conceptual foundations of innovative development of enterprises

The object of research - the processes of ensuring the development of enterprises on an innovative basis.

Research method - theoretical and logical substantiation of enterprise development processes on the basis of activation of innovation processes.

The definition of economic development is investigated in the article and the approaches concerning features of innovative development of the enterprises are substantiated.

KEYWORDS: ENTERPRISE, ENTERPRISE DEVELOPMENT, INNOVATIONS, INNOVATIVE DEVELOPMENT, FACTORS OF INNOVATIVE DEVELOPMENT.
